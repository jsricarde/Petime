You can help a pet to find a home

Petime is a mobile application for help the pets that need find a Home through the adoption.

Post a pet

Post a pets registering all data referent to pet, like color, size, name, age, country, city .

Search pets nearly you

You can search pets for adopt near of you, display a list of pets with about info pet.

Speak with the rescue

Through the real time chat you can communicate all the time with the rescue pet for adopt pet information.
